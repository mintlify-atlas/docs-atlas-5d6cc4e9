## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use Duix Avatar for my YouTube channel?">
    **Yes**, as long as you have fewer than 1,000 Monthly Active Users (subscribers who interact with your content monthly).
    
    If your channel grows beyond 1,000 MAU, you'll need to request a commercial license.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I sell videos created with Duix Avatar?">
    **Yes**, you can sell individual videos or offer video production services, as long as your total Monthly Active Users stay below 1,000.
    
    Example: Freelance video creator selling custom avatar videos to clients.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What counts as a Monthly Active User (MAU)?">
    **MAU** = Unique users who interact with your product/service incorporating Duix Avatar at least once during a calendar month.
    
    **Examples:**
    - YouTube channel: Subscribers who watch your videos
    - App: Users who open/use the app
    - Website: Unique visitors who interact with avatar content
    - SaaS: Active customers using the service
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need a commercial license for internal company use?">
    It depends on your company size:
    
    - **Under 1,000 MAU across all company products:** No license needed
    - **Over 1,000 MAU:** Commercial license required
    
    Contact DUIX.COM to discuss your specific use case.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I modify the source code?">
    **Yes!** The license grants you rights to:
    - Use and modify the code
    - Create derivative works
    - Redistribute modifications
    
    However, redistributions must:
    - Include the original license
    - Display "Built with DUIX.COM"
    - Retain attribution notices
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I breach the license?">
    DUIX.COM may terminate your license if you breach any terms.
    
    Upon termination:
    - You must delete all DUIX.COM Materials
    - Cease use immediately
    - Certain sections survive termination (warranties, liability, etc.)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Duix Avatar in open-source projects?">
    **Yes**, with conditions:
    
    - You must include the DUIX.COM license
    - Display "Built with DUIX.COM" attribution
    - Your project can use any license, but DUIX.COM Materials remain under this license
    - If your project grows beyond 1,000 MAU, commercial license needed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
